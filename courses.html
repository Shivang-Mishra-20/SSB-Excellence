Course Details

Get complete information about our flagship SSB preparation program.

About This Course

Our SSB Advanced Course is the most comprehensive preparation program available, designed by ex-SSB assessors to give you the best chance of success in your SSB interview. This 6-week intensive program covers all aspects of the SSB interview process with equal emphasis on psychological tests, GTO tasks, and personal interview.

The course is structured to progressively build your skills and confidence, starting with fundamental concepts and moving to advanced techniques used by successful candidates. You'll receive personalized feedback at every stage to help you identify and improve your weak areas.

Who Should Attend

This course is ideal for:

  • First-time SSB aspirants who want thorough preparation
  • Candidates who have previously screened out and want to improve
  • Those who want to develop officer-like qualities beyond just SSB preparation
  • Aspiring candidates for all defense services - Army, Navy, Air Force

What You'll Learn

  • Master psychological tests (TAT, WAT, SRT) with proven techniques
  • Excel in all GTO tasks including GD, GPE, PGT, HGT and Command Tasks
  • Develop a winning strategy for your personal interview
  • Understand what assessors look for in candidates
  • Build confidence through realistic mock tests and feedback

Course Curriculum

Our structured curriculum covers all aspects of the SSB interview process:

Week 1: Orientation & Psychological Tests

  • Understanding SSB process and expectations
  • Officer Like Qualities (OLQs) and how to demonstrate them
  • Thematic Apperception Test (TAT) techniques
  • Word Association Test (WAT) strategies

Week 2: Psychological Tests Continued

  • Situation Reaction Test (SRT) mastery
  • Self Description techniques
  • Psychological profiling and analysis
  • Practice tests with expert evaluation

Week 3: Group Testing Officer Tasks

  • Group Discussion strategies
  • Group Planning Exercise techniques
  • Progressive Group Task fundamentals
  • Group Obstacle Race preparation

Week 4: Advanced GTO Tasks

  • Half Group Task strategies
  • Command Task mastery
  • Individual Obstacles techniques
  • Lecturette preparation and delivery

Week 5: Personal Interview

  • Interview preparation and common questions
  • Body language and communication skills
  • Personal story development
  • Mock interviews with feedback

Week 6: Final Preparation

  • Full mock SSB interviews
  • Performance analysis and improvement plan
  • Final tips and strategies
  • Q&A with experienced assessors
