Our Story

Founded by Col VK Singh (NDA Commissioned retired Army Officer, ex SSB Pryagraj Psychologist) & English coach and mentor Nandita Singh (MA, Delhi University; MPhil, JNU; BEd, Annamalai University), SSB Excellence has been transforming defence aspirants into commissioned officers.

SSB EXCELLENCE was born out of a shared passion for mentoring young aspirants to achieve their dreams of serving the nation. Our founders, with their combined experience in the armed forces, premiere educational institutes and corporate security consultation, recognized the need for specialized training that goes beyond traditional coaching. Our unique methodology combines psychological insights with practical training along with rigorous practice sessions to bring out officer like qualities in every candidate.

Our Core Values

These fundamental beliefs guide our approach to training and define our institutional culture.

Integrity
